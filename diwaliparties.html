Diwali Festival

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is one of the most popular festivals celebrated across India and many parts of the world. The festival signifies the victory of light over darkness, knowledge over ignorance, and good over evil. Homes and temples are illuminated with traditional oil lamps called "diyas", and streets are decorated with colorful lights and rangolis. The festival is marked by family gatherings, delicious feasts, and grand displays of fireworks. It’s a time to spread happiness and prosperity, to start afresh, and to bring positivity into our lives.
